These are regular blocks. They come in two flavors. They'll do the pushing for you.

These are jumpblocks. They also come in two flavors. You have to jump yourself while on them.

These are antiblocks. They'll push you away from themselves.

These are bad, bad circles. They are enemies of us squares. Shoot em' or pass em'!
