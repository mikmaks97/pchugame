You are a pchu and your friend is a pchu. Press the shoot button to find out why. You want to climb up and up and up to reach the edge of the universe, but in order for you two lovely pchu evoking creatures to do that, you need to work together and have perfect chemistry because just like Yin and Yang, there is a part of one inside the other and you rely on each other.
